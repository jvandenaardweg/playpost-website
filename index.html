Instant podcasts of every article

Turn every article on the web into audio using industry leading high quality voices. So you can listen to it just like Podcasts and Audiobooks.

Features

Listen on the go

Listen to articles during your daily commute to work, or when doing other tasks. Just put in your earphones and press play.

High quality voices

Industry leading high quality voices are used to automatically transform text into speech.

Customize voices
Pro

Choose between a variety of high quality male and female voices with accents like American, British or Australian English.

Create your own playlist

A share button is added in every app on your phone to share articles to your playlist. Just open your article and press share.

Advanced optimization

The article’s text is optimized to be spoken to you. Correct pauses and emphasis are added for easy listening.

Offline usage

The articles audio can be downloaded and listened offline. So you can even listen to it when you have no internet connection.

The story

We have noticed the web is full of interesting long read articles that go in depth on a certain subject. If your interest in these articles is high, then you probably wished you had more time to read during the day. Reality is; we also do other things than sitting and reading. We commute. We drive. We walk. We cycle. We do our everyday chores.

What if you could just put in your earphones and listen to those articles?

Now you can!

The app uses the most advanced text to speech synthesis solution to speak text to you and sound natural and pleasant to listen to. To make the experience more personal you can choose between a variety of voices.
